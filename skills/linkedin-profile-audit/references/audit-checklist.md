# LinkedIn Profile Audit Checklist

## 1. Visual Branding
- [ ] **Profile Photo**: High-quality, professional headshot, clear background.
- [ ] **Background Banner**: Custom-designed (1584 x 396 px). Should represent industry, personal brand, or contact info.
- [ ] **Custom URL**: Clean URL (linkedin.com/in/name) without random numbers.

## 2. Headline & Bio
- [ ] **Headline**: Beyond just a job title. Includes value proposition and keywords.
- [ ] **About Section**: Written in the first person. Hooks the reader in the first 3 lines. Includes a CTA.

## 3. Experience & Education
- [ ] **Current Role**: Clearly defined with 3-5 bullet points focusing on results/metrics.
- [ ] **Company Links**: All roles are correctly linked to official company pages (showing logos).
- [ ] **Education**: Degrees listed with relevant honors or activities.

## 4. Skills & Social Proof
- [ ] **Top Skills**: Top 3 skills are pinned and relevant to the current career goal.
- [ ] **Recommendations**: At least 2-3 received recommendations from the last 2 years.

## 5. Contact Info
- [ ] **Accessibility**: Professional email, website/portfolio link, and (optional) phone number visible to connections.
