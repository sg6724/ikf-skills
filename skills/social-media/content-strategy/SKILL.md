# Content Strategy Skill

Use this skill to turn research into a plan of action.

## Process

1.  **Define Content Pillars**
    *   Create 3-4 core themes (Pillars) the company should talk about.
    *   Ensure they align with the *Intersection* of:
        *   Audience Pain Points (from Audience Analysis)
        *   Company Expertise (from Company Research)

2.  **Platform Strategy (LinkedIn Focus)**
    *   **Profile Optimization**: Recommendations for the main company page or founder profile.
    *   **Content Mix**: Ratio of Educational vs. Promotional vs. Culture content.
    *   **Formats**: Text-only, Carousel, Video, Polls.

3.  **Ideation**
    *   Generate 5 concrete post ideas per pillar.
    *   Write *hooks* for the top ideas.
